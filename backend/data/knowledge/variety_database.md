# Tomato Variety Database

## Hybrid Varieties

### 1. Arka Rakshak (IIHR)
- **Type**: Semi-determinate hybrid
- **Season**: Kharif and Rabi
- **Climate**: Tropical and subtropical (20-30°C optimal)
- **Soil**: Well-drained loamy soil, pH 6.0-7.5
- **Days to Harvest**: 65-70 days after transplanting
- **Yield**: 75-80 tons/hectare
- **Fruit**: Round, firm, 80-100g, good shelf life (15-20 days)
- **Resistance**: Bacterial wilt, early blight, leaf curl virus (Ty-2, Ty-3 genes)
- **Best For**: Areas with bacterial wilt prevalence, open-field cultivation
- **Seed Cost**: ₹800-1200 per 10g packet

### 2. Arka Samrat (IIHR)
- **Type**: Semi-determinate hybrid
- **Season**: All seasons
- **Climate**: Wide adaptability (18-35°C)
- **Soil**: All soil types with good drainage
- **Days to Harvest**: 60-65 days after transplanting
- **Yield**: 80-90 tons/hectare
- **Fruit**: Oval, deep red, 90-110g
- **Resistance**: Triple disease resistant (bacterial wilt, early blight, TYLCV)
- **Best For**: High-yield commercial farming
- **Seed Cost**: ₹1000-1500 per 10g packet

### 3. US 1143 (US Agriseeds)
- **Type**: Determinate hybrid
- **Season**: Rabi and Summer
- **Climate**: Hot climate tolerant (up to 38°C)
- **Soil**: Sandy loam to clay loam
- **Days to Harvest**: 55-60 days
- **Yield**: 55-65 tons/hectare
- **Fruit**: Square-round, 100-120g, excellent firmness for transport
- **Resistance**: Fusarium wilt, TYLCV
- **Best For**: Long-distance market supply, summer cultivation

### 4. Abhinav (Namdhari Seeds - NS 501)
- **Type**: Indeterminate hybrid
- **Season**: Rabi (protected cultivation)
- **Climate**: Moderate (18-28°C)
- **Soil**: Rich loamy soil with high organic matter
- **Days to Harvest**: 70-75 days
- **Yield**: 100-120 tons/hectare (with staking)
- **Fruit**: Round, 120-150g, cluster bearing
- **Resistance**: Moderate disease resistance
- **Best For**: Polyhouse/shade net cultivation, premium market

## Open-Pollinated Varieties

### 5. Pusa Ruby (IARI)
- **Type**: Determinate, open-pollinated
- **Season**: Rabi
- **Climate**: Cool to moderate (15-25°C)
- **Days to Harvest**: 60-65 days
- **Yield**: 25-30 tons/hectare
- **Fruit**: Round, medium (60-80g), soft
- **Advantage**: Farmer can save seeds, low input cost
- **Best For**: Small/marginal farmers, home gardens, organic farming

## Variety Selection Guide

### By Climate Zone
| Climate | Recommended Varieties |
|---------|---------------------|
| Hot Tropical (>30°C) | US 1143, Arka Rakshak |
| Subtropical (20-30°C) | Arka Samrat, Arka Rakshak |
| Cool/Hill Areas (<20°C) | Pusa Ruby, Abhinav |

### By Market Purpose
| Purpose | Recommended Varieties |
|---------|---------------------|
| Fresh market (local) | Arka Samrat, Pusa Ruby |
| Long-distance transport | US 1143, Arka Rakshak |
| Processing (sauce/paste) | Arka Rakshak |
| Premium/Export | Abhinav (NS 501) |

### By Budget
| Budget | Recommendation |
|--------|---------------|
| Low (< ₹20k/acre) | Pusa Ruby (OP, save seeds) |
| Medium (₹20-50k/acre) | Arka Rakshak, US 1143 |
| High (> ₹50k/acre) | Arka Samrat, Abhinav (polyhouse) |
